Role Introduction

class 0

Freedom Wizard

  Just a wizard want to get free, but he doesn't have any special skills. The only way to help him get special skills is own our NFT skills so that Freedom Wizard will be happy.

  This wizard is the basic wizard if you doesn't have our NFT roles.

class 1

All the class 1 roles can choose two portable skills, and they all have their special ability.

space wizard

  Space wizard has special space ability, when he release his ultimate skill, the coin's position will be forced to refresh to another romdom point.

hidden wizard

  Hidden wizard can confuse enemy's vision let them can't see him, but he can't hidden his smoke under the robe. Notice that during invisibility, hidden wizard still can be attack.

imprison wizard

  Imprison wizard like "bind play" with everyone, he can't control himself to bind other wizards. Be careful his imprison magic, if you are hit, you can move and attack for 4 seconds.

class 2

All the class 2 roles can choose two portable skills, and they all have their special ability.

You need to collect two specific mystery fragments to synthesis one class 2 role.

finagle wizard

  Finagle wizard was a habitual fraudster, so he has the ability to make a lot of fake money.

  His special ability is that he can make some fake money around the real money, and random position at the map. If another player touch the fake money, it'll be returned to respawn point.

lost wizard

  Lost wizard was a waterman, because his bad sense of direction, he can't become a captain which is his regret.

  His special ability is that he can interference other players' compass invalidate for 10 seconds.

transfer wizard

  Transfer wizard was a scientist, he studied the relation of time and space during his lifetime, he relies on his own time machine to transfer to this world.

  Transfer wizard's special ability is that he can transfer other players to a random point at the map, but he can't control where everyone should be tranfer to.
